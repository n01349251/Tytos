Things to do about Plastic Pollution Problem

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle


  • Buy your drinks in glass or aluminum containers (75% of aluminum cans are recycled only 36% of plastic bottles).
  • Use your own mug or cup for your coffee/tea
  • Reuse plastic utensils and containers
  • Make plastic bags into jump ropes, rugs, and hats

Plastic Bottle Reduction Campaign

We all need to promote non-plastic water bottles such as; stainless steel or glass water bottles that people can use them again and again, saving hundreds, if not thousands of plastic water bottles being consumed. .There are a range of bottles that are available at this Eco-Store.

Compostable Plastics Campaign

If we need to use plastic, we need to make them compostable. There are currently some researches going on with several bio-plastic companies the ways in which we can make compostable plastics more affordable and more available. A range from Bio-Bag is available at this Eco-Store.
